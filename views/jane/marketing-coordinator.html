Marketing Coordinator

Career Competencies & Core Functions · A reference map for applications & interviews

Role in one line
The engine that keeps campaigns moving
Plans, produces, and tracks marketing activity across channels so the brand reaches the right people with the right message on time.

How to use this in interviews

  • Pick one competency per pillar and tie it to a STAR story (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Quantify impact: open rates, engagement lift, leads generated, campaigns shipped, time saved.
  • Map your examples to the keywords in the job description before each interview.
  • Lead with the pillar the role emphasizes most (e.g. social-heavy → Digital & Content).
