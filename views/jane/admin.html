Administrative Professional

Career Competencies & Core Functions · A reference map for applications & interviews

Role in one line
The operational backbone of a team
Keeps people, information, schedules, and resources running smoothly so the organization can focus on its work.

How to use this in interviews

  • Pick one competency per pillar and tie it to a STAR story (Situation, Task, Action, Result).
  • Quantify impact: hours saved, error rate reduced, events run, people supported.
  • Map your examples to the keywords in the job description before each interview.
  • Lead with the pillar the role emphasizes most (e.g. exec support → Communication & Organization).
